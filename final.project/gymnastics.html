Sample Push Day

AMRAP10:

    :30 partner handstand hold (kick up, from plank)
    3 wall back bends
    :30 ring plank hold (neutral, ring turn out, forward lean)
    :30 pvc pipe overhead squat hold
    :30 arch hold/rocks
    :30 hollow hold/rocks
    :30 tripod hold
    :30 parallette support (tuck sit, L-sit, Floor: L-sit, straddle hold)

20 MIN TO COMPLETE:

LEVEL 1
    3 sets of A then B:
    A1. handstand hold x :30 (wall climb as close as possible, kick up as close as possible)
    A2. parallette shoot throughs x 10
(:30 seated German stretch between sets)
    B1. :30 bottom of ring support + :30 top of ring support
    B2. push ups x 10-15 (incline on box as needed, on floor, horizontal on rings) (5 Cuban press LAX ball pec smash/arm in between sets)
LEVEL 2
    3 sets of A then B:
    A2. 1 forward roll (10 compression lifts, :10 tuck shoulder stand) + 5 strict ring dips + 5 kipping ring dips (:30 seated German stretch between sets)
    B1. wall runs x 30 (wall facing, kick up, 3 handstand walk attempts)
    B2. tripod hip extensions x 10 (accumulate, UB, wall facing HSPUs, freestanding HSPUs)

Sample Pull Day

AMRAP10:

    :30 supinated dead hang
    5 wall Y's
    10 glute bridges
    10 scap pull ups
    :10 single arm hang/arm
    3 PVC pipe turn overs/arm
    duck walk
    crab walk
    5 candlesticks (holding onto pole, roll to seated, roll to standing)

20 MIN TO COMPLETE:

LEVEL 1
    3 sets of A then B:
    A1. faux BB pull ups x 10
    OR
    A1. strict pull ups x 8 (accumulate, UB, hollow)
    A2. straight arm ring rows x 10 (incline as needed) (:30 PVC pullover stretch in between sets)
    B1. inverted supinated chest to barbell rows x 8 (incline as needed)
    B2. around the world x 3 each direction (:30 LAX ball lat smash/side in between sets)
LEVEL 2
    3 sets of A then B:
    A1. false grip strict ring pull up w/ :10-:20 hold x 2 (accumulate time, accumulate reps, UB)
    OR
    A1. strict ring MU + negative x 2-4 (spot, accumulate, UB, weighted)
    A2. strict chest to bar pull ups x 6-8 (barbell, accumulate, UB, weighted) (:30 PVC pullover stretch in between sets)
    B1. pull-up pullover + negative x 3-5 (lean away pull-ups, spot, accumulate, UB)
    B2. straight arm ring rows x 10 (incline as needed) (:30 LAX ball lat smash/side in between sets)
