WOD

What is it? Workout of the Day

So, now that you know what it stands for...here are some examples!

Endurance Based

Every 10 Minutes x 3

    500/420M Row
    3 Wall Climbs
    10 Burpees over the erg
    2 Wall Climbs
    10 Alt D/KB Snatch
    20 AKBS
    250m run

EMOM (Every minute on the minute)

Every minute, on the minute, for 30 minutes (5 sets of each):

    Minute 1 – 5 Strict pull ups/faux bar pull ups + 5 strict T2B/V-up
    Minute 2 – 50 Double unders
    Minute 3 – 10 L-seated KB/DB strict press
    Minute 4 – 45 Second side plank (right side)
    Minute 5 – 5 Box jumps (30/24)
    Minute 6 – 45 Second side plank (left side)

Partner WOD

In teams of two, complete as many rounds and reps as possible in 25 minutes of:

    5 Power cleans (155/105)
    200 Meter Run
    20/15 Calorie Row
    - Partner 1 does 5 power cleans, then Partner 2 does 5 power cleans.
    - Partner 1 runs 200 meters, then Partner 2 runs 200 meters.
    - Partner 1 rows 20/15 calories, then Partner 2 row 20/15 calories"

Chipper Style

Complete the following chipper for time:

    500 Meter row
    40 Kettlebell Swings (53/35)
    30 Burpees
    20 Pull-Ups/Difficult jumping pull ups
    10 Deadlifts (225/155)
    20 Pull-Ups/Difficult jumping pull ups
    30 Burpees
    40 Kettlebell Swings
    500 Meter row
